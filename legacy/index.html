Background & Philosophy

Born and raised in the Lyon region of France, I was immersed in the world of gastronomy from a young age. My grandmother's kitchen was my first classroom, where I learned the importance of quality ingredients and time-honored techniques.

My culinary philosophy centers on three core principles: seasonality, simplicity, and storytelling. I believe that each dish should not only delight the palate but also convey a narrative about its origins and the hands that crafted it.

Today, I bring this approach to private dining experiences, creating bespoke menus that reflect both my classical training and innovative spirit.
