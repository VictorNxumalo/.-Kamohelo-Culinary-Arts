ingredients:
  - "120 g dark chocolate (70%), chopped"
  - "80 g unsalted butter, plus extra for moulds"
  - "2 whole eggs"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "60 g caster sugar"
  - "30 g plain flour"
  - "Pinch of fine salt"
  - "Cocoa powder for dusting"
  - "Vanilla ice cream or crème anglaise, to serve"
method:
  - "Butter four ramekins and dust with cocoa. Preheat oven to 200°C."
  - "Melt chocolate and butter gently over a bain-marie; stir until smooth. Cool slightly."
  - "Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar until pale and thick. Fold in chocolate mixture, then sift in flour and salt."
  - "Divide batter between moulds. Chill 15 minutes if time allows (helps set edges)."
  - "Bake 10–12 minutes until tops are set but centres still wobble."
  - "Rest 1 minute, invert onto plates. Serve immediately with ice cream."
