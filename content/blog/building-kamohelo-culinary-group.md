Kamohelo Culinary Arts is the visual identity guests see. Kamohelo Culinary Group is the structure behind it — the umbrella for services, concepts, and future ventures that share one culinary philosophy.

## More than a personal brand

The goal is not to remain a single-chef operation forever. The group model allows:

- **Private Chef Division** — intimate, high-touch dining experiences.
- **Catering Division** — scaled events with the same standard of craft.
- **Consulting Division** — menu and kitchen strategy for hospitality businesses.
- **Ghost kitchen concepts** — delivery brands built for modern urban dining.

Explore the full portfolio on the [Businesses & Ventures hub](/businesses).

## Entrepreneurship meets craft

I believe the next generation of chefs must think like operators as well as artisans. Recipe development, brand storytelling, and operational discipline belong in the same conversation as flavour and plating.

## What is in development

Academy programmes, cookbooks, and product lines remain on the roadmap — each documented as future ventures until they are ready to launch.
