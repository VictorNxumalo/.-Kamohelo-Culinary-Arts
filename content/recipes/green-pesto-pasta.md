ingredients:
  - "400 g dried pasta (linguine or spaghetti)"
  - "60 g pine nuts, lightly toasted"
  - "2 cups fresh basil leaves"
  - "1 clove garlic"
  - "60 g Parmesan, grated"
  - "120 ml extra virgin olive oil"
  - "Salt and black pepper"
  - "Cherry tomatoes or rocket, optional garnish"
method:
  - "Cook pasta in well-salted boiling water until al dente; reserve 1 cup pasta water."
  - "Blend basil, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan. Stream in oil to form a loose pesto. Season."
  - "Toss hot pasta with pesto, loosening with pasta water as needed."
  - "Plate and garnish with extra Parmesan, cracked pepper, and optional tomatoes or rocket."
