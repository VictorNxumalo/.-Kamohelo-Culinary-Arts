ingredients:
  - "4 chicken breasts, butterflied and pounded even"
  - "200 g spinach, wilted and squeezed dry"
  - "100 g cream cheese or ricotta"
  - "2 tbsp sun-dried tomatoes, chopped"
  - "Kitchen twine or butcher's netting"
  - "2 tbsp olive oil"
  - "300 ml chicken stock"
  - "100 ml cream"
  - "Mashed potato and glazed vegetables, to serve"
method:
  - "Season chicken. Spread filling over each breast; roll tightly and secure with twine."
  - "Sear roulades in oil until golden on all sides. Transfer to oven at 180°C."
  - "Roast until core reaches 74°C, resting 5 minutes before slicing."
  - "Deglaze pan with stock; reduce and finish with cream for sauce."
  - "Slice roulades on a bias; plate with potato, vegetables, and sauce."
