ingredients:
  - "2 white fish fillets (180–200 g each), skin on if available"
  - "2 tbsp olive oil"
  - "1 tbsp butter"
  - "150 g couscous, cooked and fluffed"
  - "1 lemon, zest and segments"
  - "Fresh dill and parsley"
  - "Salt and white pepper"
method:
  - "Pat fish dry; score skin lightly if present. Season both sides."
  - "Heat oil in a heavy pan over medium-high heat. Place fish skin-side down; press gently for even contact."
  - "Cook without moving until skin is crisp and releases easily, 3–4 minutes. Flip; add butter and baste 1–2 minutes."
  - "Fold citrus zest, herbs, and seasoning through warm couscous."
  - "Plate couscous, top with fish, and garnish with lemon segments and herb oil if using."
