ingredients:
  - "2 frenched racks of lamb (8 bones each), trimmed"
  - "3 tbsp Dijon mustard"
  - "2 tbsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped"
  - "2 tbsp fresh thyme leaves"
  - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
  - "50 g panko breadcrumbs"
  - "30 g Parmesan, finely grated"
  - "3 tbsp olive oil"
  - "Salt and cracked black pepper"
  - "500 g baby potatoes, halved"
  - "Green herb sauce (see technique notes)"
method:
  - "Bring lamb to room temperature. Season generously with salt and pepper."
  - "Sear racks in a hot oiled pan on all sides until deeply coloured. Rest briefly."
  - "Brush mustard over the fat cap. Combine herbs, garlic, breadcrumbs, and Parmesan; press onto the mustard-coated surface."
  - "Roast potatoes with olive oil and salt at 200°C until golden and tender."
  - "Roast lamb at 190°C to desired doneness (medium-rare ~18–20 min). Rest 8 minutes before carving."
  - "Slice between bones, plate with potatoes and herb sauce. Finish with flaky salt."
