Menu development is where creativity meets constraint. A beautiful idea must survive costing, prep time, skill level on the line, and the expectations of your target guest.

## Start with identity

Before writing a dish, I ask:

- Who is this for — fine dining, comfort, delivery, or event catering?
- What story does the plate tell?
- Can it be executed consistently at volume?

## Prototype, taste, refine

The portfolio on this site is evidence of that process — roulades, fondants, grazing boards. Each began as a concept, was tested, plated, and photographed only when the technique and flavour aligned.
